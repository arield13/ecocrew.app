🌍 Why EcoCrew?

EcoCrew empowers individuals to make their cities cleaner. Our mission is to encourage environmental action by rewarding users for trash reports and cleanups while providing valuable data to cities and cleanup teams.

⚙️ How It Works

📸 Report Trash

Spot waste? Snap a photo, report via app, and earn EcoPoints.

🧹 Personal Cleanup

Do the cleanup yourself and earn more points by uploading proof.

📍 Heatmaps

We highlight high-waste areas to help cities act faster and smarter.

🏆 Earn Rewards Like a Champion!

Every cleanup action you take helps the planet — and earns you EcoPoints 🌿.

Turn your contributions into real rewards! Redeem points for discounts and perks at participating eco-friendly businesses.

Exclusive Offers
